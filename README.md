# MediScan AI – 50+ Diseases Risk Analyzer

![MediScan AI Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x300.png?text=MediScan+AI)

MediScan AI is a web-based application that predicts possible diseases and analyzes health risks based on patient symptoms and vitals. It leverages a simple AI logic to compare user inputs with a database of over 50 symptoms and multiple diseases, providing **risk assessment, suggested precautions, and a visual gauge**.  

⚠️ **Disclaimer:** This tool provides AI-based predictions for informational purposes only. It is **not a substitute for professional medical advice**. Always consult a healthcare professional for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

---

## Features

- **Symptom-based disease prediction:** Select from 50+ symptoms to generate a likely disease match.  
- **Vitals analysis:** Includes temperature, heart rate, and blood pressure in risk assessment.  
- **Risk meter:** Visual gauge indicating severity level (Minor, Moderate, Critical).  
- **Dynamic 3D background:** Interactive and animated 3D particles using Three.js.  
- **Voice output:** Reads out predicted disease and risk level.  
- **Automatic diagnosis & risk reason:** Explains why a disease is predicted and risk calculated.  
- **Precautions:** Provides basic care and safety instructions based on predicted disease.  
- **Responsive design:** Works on desktop and mobile devices.
